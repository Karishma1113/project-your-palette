YourSzn

Overview

Seasonal color palettes are used to determine what kinds of colors are best suited for an individual based on their eye color, hair color, and skin tone. These color palettes are categorized into different seasons (Winter, Spring, Summer, and Fall) based on the tone of the colors in each palette.

Problem

Information on seasonal color analysis is not easily or readily accessible to all individuals, especially for those with skin tones that are less represented online and in the media. This can make the process of understanding what colors would suit them, what makeup colors to use, and how to accessorize much more challenging for certain people who do not have access to this information or are unaware of seasonal color palettes. Historically, the fashion and beauty industries have focused predominantly on groups with lighter skin tones by catering products toward these individuals. Accessibility and inclusivity has been an ongoing issue for people with darker skin tones, and with finding a personal seasonal color palette, it is generally easier for those with lighter skin tones as having a higher level of contrast against different colors can make it easier to determine complementing shades (Colour Analysis for People of Colour). Having a web app that takes hair color, skin tone and undertones, and eye color all into consideration to provide a color palette as accurate as possible would be helpful for people of all skin tones.

Existing Solutions

One solution for this problem is based off of viral TikToks where content creators go to Korea and meet with professionals to get a color analysis done in person. Unfortunately, not everyone has the money nor time to be able to fly all the way to Korea to get this done. Although there are some resources online telling what color palettes match with what skin tone, this approach is more broad and doesn't give an aspect of feeling personalized towards you specifically. In addition, factors such as skin tone and undertones are not all represented equally on websites out there (Dayblanc), and we want to ensure that people of all skin tones feel represented and catered to.

Users

The website can be accessed by anyone interested in learning more about their personalized color palette. The customizable features in this website will help promote diversity and inclusivity by providing color palettes that are inclusive of all skin tones. People of all different physical attributes can find the color palette best suited for them, which helps support minority groups that have skin tones that may not align with most mainstream fashion trends.

Features

  • The first full feature will allow users to create a personalized profile page where they can customize their own personal color palettes by adding their favorite colors and shades. Users will be able to save this information to their profile page with an added sign in feature that will prompt the user to log in to their account.
  • The second full feature will be an interaction feature that enables users to view and interact with color palettes made by other users. Users will have the option to like, share, and comment on these palettes.
  • A half feature on our app will be an interactive quiz where the user fills out questions regarding their physical attributes (skin tone, undertone, eye color, hair color) by selecting answers that apply to them. After completing the quiz, users will receive their results which will be the seasonal color palette best suited for them based on their answers. The results will provide the name of the color palette, a description, and a few example color shades part of the palette. The user can click on an expand option to see all the colors that are included in the palette.
  • Another half feature is a page dedicated to viewing all the possible season color palettes so the user can learn about all of them. On this page, the user can filter based on cool vs. warm palettes. Within the palettes, the user can also filter for specific colors, for example, just the pinks or the greens.

Information

Our web app will enable users to explore their personalized color pattern based on their eye color, hair color, and skin tone. First, we have a quiz in which users will be able input their colors and the app will provide results back to the user. Second, we have a gallery that shows popular color patterns for each eason, the users will be able to choose the specific season they want to look at. Third, for users' convenience, they will be able to compare two color patterns next to each other at the same time. The users will be viewing the colors we generated based on their eye color, hair color, and skin tone. We will code and store the results for different variables. We might use an API to display this information if it is available online, otherwise we can create a class and store these objects ourselves.

Solution

Our website will allow users who have trouble finding clothes and accessories that are best suited for their skin tone by providing them with personalized color palettes. This app can also help facilitate users' shopping decisions since it enables users to spend less time and money on clothes in order to find the right color combinations that fits their personal style. The features in our website will promote equity and inclusivity by catering to users of all appearances.
